### General Assessment

**1. Working Environment**
The working environment is friendly and comfortable, as long as everyone maintains boundaries and does not violate general rules. The workspace is quite pleasant (especially the area at the end of the tables), and the company also provides a very convenient lunch area.

**2. Support from mentors / team admins**
Whenever encountering difficulties or having questions, I just needed to proactively ask, and the seniors were always ready to support and explain thoroughly until I truly understood the issue. Especially when I asked for a project review, they provided very detailed feedback, introduced some other supporting services, and guided me on how to argue and explain the reasons when choosing features for the system.

**3. Alignment between work and major**
Since my major is Software Engineering, to meet the job requirements, I had to invest a lot of my own time to learn more about the Cloud sector. This process required me to continuously experiment and compare running on an actual Cloud environment versus a local environment.

**4. Opportunities for learning & skill development**
During the internship, I had the opportunity to listen to a lot of practical experience from the corporate environment through speakers at networking events. More importantly, I practiced and developed problem-solving skills, team management skills, as well as built a habit of proactively exploring and learning new knowledge from scratch.

**5. Culture & team spirit**
The work culture here emphasizes independence: everyone focuses on their own work, does not interfere with others' business, and just needs to strictly follow the rules. For me, being lucky enough to meet an acquaintance to sit with made the work atmosphere more joyful, reducing boredom and pressure compared to the feeling of having to "sit all alone".

**6. Policies / benefits for interns**
The company provides an internship allowance and offers flexible time conditions when interns have necessary personal matters. Additionally, being given the opportunity to participate in professional experience sharing sessions is a huge plus for me during this internship.
