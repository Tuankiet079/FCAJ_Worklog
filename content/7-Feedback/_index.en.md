### General Reflections & Impressions

**1. Working Environment**
The working environment at the company is highly friendly, open, and comfortable while maintaining strong respect for common guidelines. The workspace is well-designed and pleasant (especially the work area near the back), accompanied by convenient lunch amenities that provide great comfort for interns.

**2. Support from Mentors and the Admin Team**
Whenever I encountered obstacles or technical challenges, simply reaching out proactively allowed me to receive enthusiastic support and detailed explanations from mentors. Notably, during project reviews, the mentors provided exceptionally thorough feedback, suggested useful AWS services, and guided me on logical reasoning when making architectural choices for the system.

**3. Relevance Between Practical Tasks and Field of Study**
Coming from a Software Engineering background, I dedicated significant personal effort to studying and acquiring deeper knowledge in Cloud Computing to meet project requirements effectively. This process required persistence in testing, benchmarking, and continuously contrasting real cloud environments with local environments.

**4. Opportunities for Growth and Learning**
This internship granted me valuable opportunities to gain practical experience through insights shared by speakers at industry engagement events. More importantly, I honed my problem-solving mindset, elevated my teamwork skills, and cultivated habits of proactive research to grasp new technologies from foundational principles upward.

**5. Corporate Culture and Team Spirit**
The workplace culture emphasizes self-discipline and personal initiative: each team member remains deeply focused on their responsibilities, respects personal boundaries, and adheres to shared rules. Working alongside familiar peers in a professional setting helped me adapt quickly, creating a positive, enjoyable working atmosphere that greatly reduced stress throughout the internship.

**6. Intern Policies and Benefits**
The company provides thoughtful internship allowance policies and offers flexible scheduling when interns need to handle necessary personal matters. Furthermore, continuous opportunities to attend technical sharing sessions proved to be a major advantage, delivering substantial real-world value during this internship.
